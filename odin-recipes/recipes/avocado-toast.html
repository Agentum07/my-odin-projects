Avocado Toast

Description

Elevate your breakfast game with Avocado Toast, a simple yet satisfying dish that has taken the world by storm. Creamy, ripe avocados mashed onto crispy toast provide a wholesome and delightful start to your day.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole-grain or artisanal bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 small lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 poached or fried eggs (optional, for added protein)
  • Fresh herbs, like cilantro or parsley, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Start by toasting your bread until it's perfectly crispy. You can use a toaster, oven, or stovetop to achieve your desired level of crispness.
  2. While the toast is cooking, cut the ripe avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash it with a fork until you achieve your preferred level of creaminess.
  3. Squeeze the juice of half a lemon over the mashed avocado to add a fresh and zesty flavor. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and add a pinch of red pepper flakes for a hint of heat if desired.
  4. Once the toast is ready, spread the creamy avocado mixture generously on each slice.
  5. If you'd like to add some protein, consider topping your avocado toast with poached or fried eggs.
  6. Garnish your avocado toast with fresh herbs like cilantro or parsley for a burst of color and extra freshness.
  7. Serve your Avocado Toast immediately and savor the contrast of creamy, tangy avocado and crispy toast, a delightful breakfast treat that's both nutritious and delicious.

Enjoy your avocado toast!
